About Ahmed Shire

Ahmed Shire is a 23-year-old photographer from London, England. He began his journey into photography at the age of 15, working with second-hand cameras and teaching himself the craft through experience.

Today, Ahmed shoots on a Sony ZV-E10, capturing candid moments, vibrant street scenes, and creative portraits. His work reflects a blend of storytelling, emotion, and urban exploration.

When not behind the lens, Ahmed is exploring new locations across London for his next shoot or mentoring aspiring photographers online.
